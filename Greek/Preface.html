Greek

I'm working towards reading 1 John initially. That requires beginning greek and then a little bit of vocabulary after that. Three video series to reference on 1 John: Greek Bible Speaker (Eras. Gk) by Ted Hildebrandt Biblicalelearning; 1 John 1 by Daily Dose of Greek; 1 John in Greek by Ken Schenck.

Gresham Machen's "New Testament Greek for Beginners" (1923) is in the public domain, so I might pull from it to explain each section as I get to it.

The grammar I'm using is Beginning with New Testament Greek by Benjamin L Merkle and Robert L. Plummer. The website for the textbook contains a lot of additional helpful materials as well: Daily Dose of Greek. I got the bundle of ios apps by Danny Zacharias which includes ParseGreek, FlashGreek, and IGreek for practice: Danny Zacharias Apps. I also got Tydale's Greek New Testament.

Bill Mounce has lectures going through the main points of Basics of Biblical Greek which might be helpful to reference: Biblical Greek.
