Math Roadmap

Fundamentals

Goal: Basic understanding of arithmetic and number theory and introduction to algebra.

Math Basics

Arithmetic and Number Theory:

  • Basic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division).
  • Factors, multiples, prime numbers, GCD, LCM.
  • Modular arithmetic basics (reading a clock).

Pre-Algebra:

  • Algebraic expressions, simplification, and factorization.
  • Linear equations, inequalities.
  • Systems of equations, quadratic equations.

Intermediate Algebra

Goal: Greater understanding of algebra and introduction to the mathematics of shapes.

Foundational

Algebra 1:

  • Coordinate plane, basic graphing, and functions.
  • Ratios, proportions, exponents, and radicals.

Algebra 2:

  • Complex numbers, polynomials, rational expressions.
  • Exponential and logarithmic functions.
  • Advanced equation solving techniques.

Geometry:

  • Points, lines, planes, angles, triangles, polygons, circles.
  • Cartesian plane, distance formula, slope, equations of lines, circles, parabolas.
  • Translations, rotations, reflections, and scaling.
  • Pythagorean theorem, congruence and similarity, basic constructions.

Trigonometry:

  • Angles, radians, and trigonometric functions.
  • Trigonometric identities and equations.
  • Applications of trigonometry in geometry and real-world problems.

Foundations of Calculus

Goal: Basic understanding of the fundamental building blocks of calculus.

Calculus Prerequisites

Pre-Calculus:

  • Coordinate plane, basic graphing, and functions.
  • Ratios, proportions, exponents, and radicals.

Limits and Continuity:

  • Complex numbers, polynomials, rational expressions.
  • Exponential and logarithmic functions.
  • Advanced equation solving techniques.

Differentiation Basics:

  • Concept of the derivative, rules of differentiation.
  • Applications of derivatives (tangent lines, optimization).
