Memory

Alphabet

Learn to sing the Greek Alphabet Song by Daily Dose of Greek. It shouldn't take more than a few days to learn the basic alphabet. The upper case letters are a bit more challenging, but you should know the lower case letters especially well. I found it helpful to remember the letters in groups of fours and then remembering which letter starts the next group of four.

Diphthongs

Diphthong Pronunciation English Equivalent Greek Word Example
αι “eye” as in aisle παῖς
αυ “ow” as in kraut αὐτός
ει “ay” as in freight εἰρήνη
ευ “yew” as in feud εὐαγγέλιον
οι “oi” as in oil οἶκος
ου “oo” as in soup οὐρανός
υι “we” as in suite υἱός
ηυ “ay-oo” (rare) “ay-OO-lo-geh-sen” ηὐλόγησεν
ωυ “ō-oo” sounded out Μωϋσῆς
ιη “ee-ay” → “yea” as in yeast Ἰησοῦς

Reading

Read 1 John along with 1 John 1 -- Greek Bible Speaker (Erasmian/Seminary) to compare and practice pronunciation. It will help with memory, reading speed, and comprehension over time if you nail down being able to read the text well.
