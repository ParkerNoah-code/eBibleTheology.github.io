Lesson 9: Personal & Relative Pronouns

Personal Pronouns

1st Person

Case Singular Plural
Nominative ἐγώ (I) ἡμεῖς (we)
Genitive μου / ἐμοῦ (of me, my) ἡμῶν (of us, our)
Dative μοι / ἐμοί (to/for me) ἡμῖν (to/for us)
Accusative με / ἐμέ (me) ἡμᾶς (us)

2nd Person

Case Singular Plural
Nominative σύ (you) ὑμεῖς (you pl.)
Genitive σου / σοῦ (of you, your) ὑμῶν (of you, your)
Dative σοι / σοί (to/for you) ὑμῖν (to/for you)
Accusative σε / σέ (you) ὑμᾶς (you)

3rd Person

Masculine

Case Singular Plural
Nominative αὐτός (he) αὐτοί (they)
Genitive αὐτοῦ (his) αὐτῶν (their)
Dative αὐτῷ (to him) αὐτοῖς (to them)
Accusative αὐτόν (him) αὐτούς (them)

Feminine

Case Singular Plural
Nominative αὐτή (she) αὐταί (they)
Genitive αὐτῆς (her) αὐτῶν (their)
Dative αὐτῇ (to her) αὐταῖς (to them)
Accusative αὐτήν (her) αὐτάς (them)

Neuter

Case Singular Plural
Nominative αὐτό (it) αὐτά (they)
Genitive αὐτοῦ (its) αὐτῶν (their)
Dative αὐτῷ (to it) αὐτοῖς (to them)
Accusative αὐτό (it) αὐτά (them)

Vocabulary

Verbs

  • διώκω – I pursue, persecute
  • δοξάζω – I glorify, praise
  • πέμπω – I send
  • πράσσω – I do, practice
  • σώζω – I save, rescue, heal
  • τηρέω – I keep, guard, obey

Nouns

  • ἀπόστολος, ὁ – apostle, messenger
  • ἐντολή, ἡ – command
  • καιρός, ὁ – time, season
  • κεφαλή, ἡ – head
  • πρόσωπον, τό – face, appearance
  • σάββατον, τό – Sabbath, week

Adverbs

  • ἔτι – still, yet, more
  • μᾶλλον – more, rather
  • οὐκέτι – no longer
