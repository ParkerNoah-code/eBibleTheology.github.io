Lesson 6: Imperfect Indicative

Meaning of the Imperfect Tense

  • Ongoing or progressive action in the past.
  • Progressive Imperfect: continuous past action (e.g., καὶ πολλοὶ πλούσιοι ἔβαλλον πολλὰ – "many rich were putting in much").
  • Inceptive Imperfect: beginning of an action (e.g., ἔστη καὶ περιεπάτει – "he stood and began to walk").
  • Iterative Imperfect: repeated or customary action (e.g., διεμαρτύρατο καὶ παρεκάλει αὐτοὺς – "he solemnly testified and kept on exhorting them").

Formation of the Imperfect Indicative

Active Indicative Endings

  • Stem + Connecting Vowel + Personal Ending + Augment (ἐ)
  • Example: λύ → ἐλύομεν ("we were loosing")
Person Ending
1st sg -ον
2nd sg -ες
3rd sg -ε(ν)
1st pl -ομεν
2nd pl -ετε
3rd pl -ον

Middle/Passive Indicative Endings

  • Stem + Connecting Vowel + Personal Ending + Augment (ἐ)
  • Example: λύ → ἐλυόμεθα ("we were being loosed")
Person Ending
1st sg -όμην
2nd sg -ου
3rd sg -ετο
1st pl -όμεθα
2nd pl -εσθε
3rd pl -οντο

Augment Rules

  • Consonant start: add ἐ (e.g., λύω → ἔλυον).
  • Vowel start: vowel lengthens:
    • ἀκούω → ἤκουον
    • ἔρχομαι → ἠρχόμην
    • ὀφείλω → ὠφείλον
  • Diphthong αι: iota subscript (e.g., αἰτοῦμαι → ᾐτούμην).
  • ἔχω (stem σεχ-): εἶχον (not ἤχον).

Use of οὐ (Negation)

  • οὐ negates a verb.
  • Before smooth breathing vowel: οὐκ (e.g., οὐκ ἔλυεν – "he was not loosing").
  • Before rough breathing vowel: οὐχ (e.g., οὐχ ὑπάγω – "I am not departing").
  • οὐχί used for emphasis: "No!"

Irregular Masculine Nouns

1st declension masculine nouns (e.g., μαθητής, προφήτης) take masculine articles and adjectives.

Example: τὸν ἀγαθὸν μαθητήν ("the good disciple")

Declension of μαθητής

Case Singular Plural
Nominative ὁ μαθητής οἱ μαθηταί
Genitive τοῦ μαθητοῦ τῶν μαθητῶν
Dative τῷ μαθητῇ τοῖς μαθηταῖς
Accusative τὸν μαθητήν τοὺς μαθητάς

Vocabulary

  • ἀγαπάω – I love
  • γεννάω – I give birth to, bear, beget
  • ἐπερωτάω, ἐρωτάω – I ask, question, request
  • αἰτέω – I ask, demand
  • ἀκολουθέω – I follow
  • ζητέω – I seek, look for
  • καλέω – I call, invite, name
  • λαλέω – I speak, say
  • μαρτυρέω – I testify, bear witness
  • παρακαλέω – I call, urge, comfort
  • περιπατέω – I walk, live
  • ποιέω – I do, make
  • φοβέομαι – I am afraid, fear, respect
  • πληρόω – I fill, fulfill, complete
